# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Content creators, social media managers, and small business owners who want to produce and publish short-form videos for TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook without friction. They're often non-technical, time-pressured, and judge a tool by how fast it gets them from idea to posted.

## Product Purpose

Beyond Social is an AI-powered short-form video creation and publishing platform. It surfaces trending ideas, generates scripts, produces videos via AI, and schedules them to social platforms — all in one flow. Success looks like a creator going from blank screen to scheduled post in under 10 minutes, repeatedly, without needing any video production skills.

## Brand Personality

Slick, minimal, fast. The tool should feel like it was built for someone who knows what they're doing and doesn't need to be hand-held. Confident without being loud.

## Emotional Goal

After finishing a video, the user should feel like a pro — like the tool did the heavy lifting and they just made the call. Effortless competence.

## Anti-references

- No generic AI video generation site aesthetics (RunwayML, Pika, HeyGen) — no dark neon, no techno-grid backgrounds, no glowing orbs
- No Canva playfulness or rainbow color systems
- No corporate SaaS blue (Hubspot, Salesforce)
- No dashboard-by-committee (Grafana, Tableau) heaviness

## References

Wan.videos landing page as a directional reference — that level of restraint and confidence — but more polished, more product-grade, and faster-feeling in interaction.

## Design Principles

1. **Compression over expansion.** Every screen should do less than the engineer's first instinct. If a step can be eliminated, eliminate it.
2. **Confidence without decoration.** The UI expresses quality through precision and restraint, not gradients or effects.
3. **Speed is a feature.** Perceived performance matters. Transitions, loading states, and empty states should all reinforce that this tool moves fast.
4. **The creator is the expert.** Don't over-explain. Trust that the user knows what a script or a niche is.
5. **One thing at a time.** The video creation flow is a single focused thread. No branching, no backtracking, no overwhelming option sets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard navigable flows. Sufficient contrast at all interactive states. Reduced motion support via `prefers-reduced-motion`.
